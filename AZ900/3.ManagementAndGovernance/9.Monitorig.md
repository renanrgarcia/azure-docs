# Monitoring
## Azure Advisor
- Azure Advisor is a personalized cloud consultant that helps you follow best practices to optimize your Azure deployments. The recommendations are provided in 5 categories:
  - `Reliability`: Ensure your applications are resilient and available.
  - `Security`: Protect your resources and data from threats.
  - `Performance`: Optimize your resources for better performance.
  - `Cost`: Manage your spending and optimize costs.
  - `Operational Excellence`: Improve your operational processes and practices.

## Azure Service Health
- Azure Service Health provides personalized alerts and guidance when Azure service issues affect you. Key features include:
  - **Service Status**: View the current status of Azure services and regions.
  - **Incident Alerts**: Receive notifications about service incidents and planned maintenance.
  - **Resource Health**: Monitor the health of your Azure resources and receive alerts for potential issues.

## Azure Monitor
- Azure Monitor is a platform for collecting data on your resources, analyzing that data, visualizing the information, and even acting on the results. Azure Monitor can monitor Azure resources, your on-premises resources, and even multi-cloud resources like virtual machines hosted with a different cloud provider. Key features include:
  - **Azure Log Analytics**: Collect and analyze log data from various sources to gain insights into your applications and infrastructure.
  - **Azure Monitor Alerts**: Set up alerts to notify you of critical conditions and take automated actions.
  - **Application Insights**: Monitor the performance and usage of your applications, providing deep insights into user behavior and application health.
