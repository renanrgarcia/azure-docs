# Microsoft Purview
- Microsoft Purview is a unified data governance solution that helps organizations manage and govern their data across various sources. Key features include:
  - **Data catalog**: Automatically discover and classify data assets across your organization.
  - **Data lineage**: Visualize the flow of data from its source to its destination, helping you understand data transformations and dependencies.
  - **Data policies**: Define and enforce data governance policies to ensure compliance and data protection.
  - **Data insights**: Gain insights into your data landscape, including data usage patterns and potential risks.

## Solution Areas
- `Risk and Compliance`: Protect sensitive data and ensure compliance with regulations.
- `Unified Data Governance`: Provide a comprehensive framework for managing data across the organization, including data discovery, classification, and policy enforcement.
