# Azure Service Trust
- Azure Service Trust is a set of tools and resources designed to help organizations manage their compliance and security requirements in the cloud. Key components include:
  - **Compliance Manager**: A tool that helps you assess and manage your compliance posture across Azure services.
  - **Trust Center**: A centralized resource for information about Azure's security, privacy, and compliance practices.
  - **Service Trust Portal**: A portal that provides access to compliance documentation, audit reports, and other resources.

## Key Features
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Azure Service Trust helps you meet various regulatory requirements, such as GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO 27001.
- **Security Assessments**: Conduct regular security assessments to identify and mitigate potential risks in your Azure environment.
- **Audit Reports**: Access detailed audit reports and compliance documentation to demonstrate your compliance posture to stakeholders.

## Benefits
- Simplified compliance management for Azure services.
- Enhanced visibility into your security and compliance posture.
- Access to a wealth of resources and tools to support your compliance efforts.
